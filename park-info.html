Park Rules

Guest Guidelines

  • Appropriate swimwear required at all times in water areas
  • Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult
  • No outside food or beverages permitted
  • Follow all ride height and health restrictions
  • Smoking only in designated areas
  • Photography allowed; drones require prior approval
  • Lockers available — do not leave valuables unattended
Safety

Your Safety is Our Priority

  • All attractions inspected daily by certified engineers
  • Water quality tested every 2 hours
  • Emergency response team on standby
  • Life jackets provided free at designated areas
  • Weather monitoring — rides close during lightning
