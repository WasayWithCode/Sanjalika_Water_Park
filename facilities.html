Facilities

Premium amenities for your comfort and convenience.

Secure Lockers

Electronic lockers in 3 sizes. Rental from $5/day with unlimited access.

Private Cabanas

Luxury cabanas with shade, seating, mini-fridge, and dedicated service. From $80/day.

Aqua Spa

Massages, facials, and wellness treatments. Book at guest services or online.

Parking

Free parking for 2,000+ vehicles. VIP covered parking available for $10.

First Aid

3 first aid stations with qualified nurses. Emergency response team on standby.

Free WiFi

High-speed WiFi throughout the park. Connect to "Sanjalika-Guest" network.

Changing Rooms

Clean, spacious changing facilities with showers, hair dryers, and family rooms.

Gift Shop

Souvenirs, swimwear, sunscreen, and Sanjalika branded merchandise.

Accessibility

Wheelchair paths, adapted restrooms, and companion ticket discounts available.
