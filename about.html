Our Story

A journey of passion, innovation, and aquatic wonder.

History

Born from a Dream

In 2010, visionary entrepreneur Amara Silva transformed 25 acres of coastal land into what would become Sri Lanka's most celebrated water park. What started as a family dream has grown into a world-class destination attracting over 500,000 visitors annually.

Named after the Sanskrit word for "lotus pond," Sanjalika embodies purity, beauty, and the transformative power of water — values that guide everything we do.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled aquatic adventures that inspire joy, foster family bonds, and create lasting memories — all while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To become Asia's most celebrated luxury water park destination by 2030, setting new benchmarks for guest experience, sustainability, and innovative aquatic entertainment.
